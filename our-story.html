The Space

This privately-owned oceanfront villa is located at the south end of Old Town and a five minute walk to restaurants and shopping. Coconut House is a two bedroom, one and a half bathroom home made of gorgeous hardwood and that sits on stilts directly over the sea. The view over the harbour and the archipelago is absolutely stunning.

Tides | Beach

The highest tides are over 3 meters high, and are at the full moon and new {black} moon phase of EACH month, EVERY month. The normal tides are around 2.5 meters and change daily every 6 hours, for more information look at Phuket’s tide chart on the internet. The sea's clarity is very much influenced by the wind and waves, and therefore the time of the season.

Other things to note

If you're looking for a serene, quiet villa miles from Thai life this is not the place. These are authentic wooden houses in a working fishing village, so walls aren’t soundproofed, neighbours are close by, and noise carries across the water. Perhaps not for honeymooners . Please be respectful of your environment, If your looking for a party, Make your own (quiet) fun or head across the island to the tourist hotspots.
